Features

Unlimited, Re-Useable Lists

With our list templates, you can create unlimited, re-usable to-lists to give you a head start whenever a new challenge arises! Is Wednesday grocery shopping day? Tell Wunderlist that you're going to need a new shopping list every Tuesday, and we'll create you one based off your template - we'll even notify you when it's created so you can get started as early as you like.

Take Your Lists With You

Wunderlist is available everywhere! Use it on your PC, and pick up those lists from your phone whilst you're about and about! Suddenly remember something you were supposed to add just before you go to bed? Pull out your tablet, update as you need to and it will all be there on your PC the next morning!

Schedule Timed Reminders

With Wunderlist you can set deadlines schedule reminders for to-do list tasks to make sure you always get things done on time! Erase the worry of last minute panic and prioritise your workload, so you can make sure you're always working on what's most important at the time!

Get Geographically Relevant Reminders

You can save certain locations in Wunderlist and get reminders based on where you are! Near your grocery store? If you've saved its location in Wunderlist, you'll get a notification reminding you that you have tasks to complete in that area. Never forget to grab that salad for dinner on your way home again!

Share Your Lists With Others

At Wunderlist, we understand that a problem shared is a problem halved. Distribution and delegation of tasks can make whatever you're working on a whole lot easier, so you can share your lists and reminders with colleagues and receive real time updates when tasks have been completed so you're never in the dark with how much there is left to do!

Lists Your Way

Not all tasks can just be one line of text. With Wunderlist, you can add attachments, images and URLS to make sure the information you need is always just a click away! Customise the look of your lists with themes, colour coding and catergorisation so you can find what you're looking for as quickly as possible!
